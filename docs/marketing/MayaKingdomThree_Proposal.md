## 1. Executive Summary

**Project Name:** Maya Kingdom Three  
**Type:** ACTION / STRATEGY / RPG  
**Platform Target:** PC (Steam), Nintendo Switch 2, Mobile (iOS/Android)  
**Target Release:** Q2 2028  
**Team:** NorthernStar Studio

**One-Liner:** "Conquer the ancient world in this epic turn-based strategy game spanning three great civilizations."

**Summary:**  
The third installment in the Maya Kingdom series takes players on a grand campaign across the ancient Americas. Lead one of three civilizations — Maya, Aztec, or Inca — through ages of growth, war, and cultural achievement. Features the largest map in series history, enhanced AI, multiplayer, and cross-platform play.

## 2. Market Opportunity

### 2.1 Target Audience

| Segment | Description | Priority |
|---------|--------------|----------|
| Primary | Strategy gamers, 18-45 | High |
| Secondary | Series fans from MK1 & MK2 | High |
| Tertiary | History buffs, 25-50 | Medium |

### 2.2 Market Fit

- **Current trends:** 4X strategy experiencing renaissance (Civilization VII, Humankind). Retro/classic style games popular on Switch.
- **Gap identified:** No major turn-based strategy game covering all of Mesoamerica AND South America with three playable civilizations.
- **Why now:** Series proven (MK1, MK2). Engine mature. Cross-platform demand growing.

### 2.3 Competitive Landscape

| Competitor | Strengths | Weaknesses | Our Differentiation |
|------------|-----------|------------|---------------------|
| Civilization VII | AAA production, brand | High cost, complex | Trilogy completion, accessible |
| Humankind | Unique culture blending | Mixed reception | Richer narrative focus |
| Old World | Quality design | Niche only | Better platform support |

## 3. Value Proposition

### Core Hook
Lead Maya, Aztec, or Inca to dominance — experience the rise and fall of ancient empires in the most ambitious entry yet.

### Key Features (MVP)
1. Three playable civilizations with unique mechanics
2. Grand campaign spanning 3,000 years
3. Enhanced tactical combat system
4. Dynamic diplomacy with rival AI
5. Trade routes and cultural exchange
6. Multiplayer (2-8 players)
7. Cross-platform play (PC, Switch 2)

### Why Players Will Love It
- Trilogy culmination — most content-rich entry
- Accessible yet deep gameplay
- Stunning art inspired by ancient Americas
- Cross-platform multiplayer
- Replayable with three distinct factions
