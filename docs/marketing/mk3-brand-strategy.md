## Executive Summary

This document outlines the brand strategy for Maya Kingdom Three, focusing on leveraging the trilogy narrative to create a compelling marketing position. The strategy builds on the existing Maya Kingdom franchise while differentiating from competitors in the historical strategy genre.

---

## Brand Positioning

### Core Message

**"The Final Chapter"** — Maya Kingdom Three is the culmination of a three-game journey, offering players the definitive experience in our Mesoamerican historical strategy series.

### Tagline Options

1. **"One World. Three Empires."** — Emphasizes the scale and the three playable civilizations
2. **"Write Your Own History"** — Player agency, narrative focus
3. **"The Final Chapter"** — Trilogy culmination, closure for invested players
4. **"Conquer the Ancient World"** — Action-oriented, grand scale

**Recommended:** "One World. Three Empires." — specific, memorable, highlights the unique three-civ feature

## Franchise Identity

### What Makes Maya Kingdom Unique

1. **Specific Focus** — Only game covering Maya + Aztec + Inca in one package
2. **Narrative Depth** — Story-driven campaigns (compete with Old World)
3. **Trilogy Investment** — Players invested in characters/outcomes from MK1 & MK2
4. **Accessible Complexity** — Deeper than casual, simpler than Civ VII

### Visual Identity

| Element | Recommendation |
|---------|----------------|
| Art Style | 2.5D isometric — warm, vibrant colors inspired by Mesoamerican art |
| Color Palette | Terracotta, jade green, gold, deep blue (water), sunset orange |
| Typography | Clean sans-serif + decorative accents for headers |
| Logo | "MK3" icon with three interwoven elements (sun, pyramid, serpent) |

## Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Trilogy confusion | Clear marketing: "You can start here or play the trilogy" |
| Competition from Civ VII | Emphasize unique Mesoamerican focus, price advantage |
| Scope concerns | Show depth through gameplay demos, not just promises |
